Project-2

Title: Vehicle Monitoring System Using Arduino

My Role:Team Leader

Project Description: In many cases, drivers who are drowsy make no effort to apply brake or avoid an accident. So, a system is designed which senses the condition of the driver (his/her health) and stops the vehicle immediately if an abnormal condition of the driver is sensed to avoid accidents. In this system an eye blink sensor, an alcohol sensor are interfaced to an NodeMCU ESP8266. If any of these sensors senses an abnormal condition of the driver, the vehicle automatically slows down and stops. A buzzer is placed in the vehicle which alerts the surrounding vehicles.
